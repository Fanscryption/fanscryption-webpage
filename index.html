About us

Welcome… to Fanscryption.

We are a community of artists and fans of Inscryption alike who come together to bring our love of the game into the real world. The 2021 game Inscryption by Daniel Mullins struck a chord with a lot of people, and it has inspired a countless number of amazing artistic projects.

It started as a small group of artists who wanted to make a physical recreation of Inscryption. However, we have grown into a large community of physical Inscryption artists, custom card designers, cosplayers, and more. We are dedicated to bringing the compelling themes and story of Inscryption into the real world through our various projects.

Come join our discord and other socials to see some of the astounding work our community has produced. Also take a look at our featured projects below! We hope you enjoy…
